# Collection of articles and related source material for Open Source Stewards

### This is a new category of legal persons introduced by the CRA. The role of Open Source Stewards distinguishes Open Source actors who do not monetize the software they produce from Manufacturers who do. It imposes a lighter regulatory burden on Stewards and reduces the risks they face in the event of non-compliance
